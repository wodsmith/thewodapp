# Quick Start

Get up and running with WodSmith in just a few minutes.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit [app.wadsmith.com](https://app.wadsmith.com) and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email and create a password, or use social login
3. Verify your email address

## Setting Up Your Gym

After signing up, you'll be guided through the initial setup:

1. **Create your team** - Enter your gym name and details
2. **Invite coaches** - Add team members who will help manage workouts
3. **Configure settings** - Set your timezone and preferences

## Your First Workout

Once your team is set up, create your first workout:

1. Navigate to **Workouts** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Workout**
3. Choose a workout type (AMRAP, For Time, EMOM, etc.)
4. Add movements and configure scoring
5. Save and publish to your team
