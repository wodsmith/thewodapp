# Set Up Your Gym on WodSmith

In this tutorial, we will set up your gym on WodSmith and create your first workout. By the end, you will have a team configured, your first coach invited, and a workout published to your calendar.

## What We'll Accomplish

1. Create your gym's team
2. Configure basic settings
3. Invite your first coach
4. Create and publish a workout

Let's get started!

## Step 1: Create Your Account

First, we need to create your WodSmith account.

1. Visit [app.wadsmith.com](https://app.wadsmith.com)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Click **Create Account**

**Notice** the welcome screen. You're now signed in and ready to create your gym's team.

## Step 2: Create Your Team

Now we'll create the team that represents your gym.

1. Click **Create Team**
2. Enter your gym's name (e.g., "CrossFit Downtown")
3. Add an optional description
4. Select your timezone

**You should see** your new team appear in the sidebar. This is now your workspace.

## Step 3: Configure Team Settings

Let's set up some basic preferences.

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select **General**
3. Review the default settings:
   - Workout visibility (public or members-only)
   - Member permissions
4. Adjust any settings as needed
5. Click **Save**

**Notice** the confirmation message. Your settings are saved.

## Step 4: Invite Your First Coach

Now we'll add a coach to help manage programming.

1. Navigate to **Settings** â†’ **Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter your coach's email address
4. Select **Coach** from the role dropdown
5. Click **Send Invitation**

**You should see** the pending invitation listed. Your coach will receive an email to join.

## Step 5: Create Your First Workout

Let's create a classic CrossFit workout together.

1. Click **Workouts** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Workout**
3. Select **AMRAP** as the workout type
4. Enter `12` for the time cap (minutes)

**Notice** the workout form updating to show AMRAP-specific options.

### Add Movements

1. Click **Add Movement**
2. Search for "Pull-up" and select it
3. Enter `10` for the rep count
4. Click **Add Movement** again
5. Search for "Push-up" and select it
6. Enter `15` for the rep count
7. Add one more: "Air Squat" with `20` reps

**You should see** three movements listed:
- 10 Pull-ups
- 15 Push-ups
- 20 Air Squats

### Save and Publish

1. Add a title: "Classic Cindy"
2. Click **Publish**

**Notice** the workout now appears in your library. Athletes can see it!

## Step 6: Schedule the Workout

Finally, let's add this workout to tomorrow's calendar.

1. Navigate to **Programming** in the sidebar
2. Click on tomorrow's date
3. Click **Add Workout**
4. Select "Classic Cindy" from the list
5. Click **Save**

**You should see** the workout appear on the calendar for tomorrow.

## You've Done It!

Congratulations! You have successfully:

- Created your gym's WodSmith team
- Configured basic settings
- Invited a coach
- Created and scheduled your first workout
