# How to Edit Competition Settings

Modify competition details, divisions, and configuration after creation.

## Prerequisites

- Competition organizer permissions

## Editing Basic Information

1. Open your competition
2. Go to **Settings** → **General**
3. Update:
   - Competition name
   - Date and time
   - Location
   - Description
4. Click **Save**

## Modifying Divisions

### Adding a Division

1. Go to **Divisions**
2. Click **Add Division**
3. Configure name and cap
4. Save

### Editing a Division

1. Click on the division
2. Modify settings
3. Save

:::caution
Changing division requirements after athletes register may require communication with affected registrants.
:::

## Updating Events

### Adding Events

1. Go to **Events**
2. Click **Add Event**
3. Configure workout details
4. Save

### Editing Events

1. Click on the event
2. Modify workout, standards, or time cap
3. Save

## Registration Settings

To modify registration:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Registration**
2. Update:
   - Open/close dates
   - Entry fees
   - Waiver requirements
   - Custom questions
3. Save

## Unpublishing a Competition

To take the competition offline temporarily:

1. Go to **Settings** → **General**
2. Toggle **Published** to off
3. Competition page becomes inaccessible
