# How to Schedule Heats

Organize athletes into heats and create your event day schedule.

## Prerequisites

- Competition with confirmed registrations
- Events configured

## Auto-Generating Heats

1. Open your competition
2. Go to **Schedule** → **Generate Heats**
3. Configure options:
   - Athletes per heat
   - Heat duration
   - Transition time
4. Click **Generate**

## Manual Heat Assignment

To manually assign athletes:

1. Go to **Schedule** → **Heats**
2. Select an event
3. Drag athletes between heats
4. Or click **Assign** on individual athletes

## Heat Configuration

For each heat:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Start Time** | When this heat begins |
| **Athletes** | Who's competing |
| **Lanes** | Lane assignments |
| **Judges** | Assigned judges (optional) |

## Adjusting the Schedule

To modify heat times:

1. Click on the heat
2. Adjust start time
3. Subsequent heats auto-adjust (if linked)

## Publishing the Schedule

When ready for athletes to view:

1. Review all heat assignments
2. Click **Publish Schedule**
3. Athletes see their heat times

## Exporting Heat Sheets

1. Go to **Schedule**
2. Click **Export** → **Heat Sheets**
3. Choose format: PDF or Print
4. Distribute to judges and volunteers
