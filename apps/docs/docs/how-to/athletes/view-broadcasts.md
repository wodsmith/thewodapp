# How to View Broadcasts

See announcements the organizer has sent to athletes.

## Viewing Announcements

1. Open the competition page.
2. Click the **Announcements** tab.

![Announcements tab with two broadcasts](/img/how-to/athletes/athlete-broadcasts.png)

Broadcasts appear in reverse chronological order — newest at the top — showing the title, message body, and timestamp.

If the organizer hasn't posted any announcements yet, you'll see an empty-state message.

## Email Notifications

When the organizer sends a broadcast with email enabled, you'll also receive the message in your inbox. The email contains the same title and body as the in-app announcement, plus a link back to the competition page.

If you don't see the emails, check your spam folder and add the organizer's sending domain to your safe senders list.

## Audience Filters

Organizers can target broadcasts to specific groups:

- **All athletes** — everyone who's registered
- **By division** — only RX athletes, or only Masters, etc.
- **All volunteers / by role** — only your judges, or only your medical staff
- **Pending invites** — teammates who haven't yet accepted their invitation
- **Public** — everyone associated with the competition

You only see broadcasts whose audience includes you. If your team captain mentions a broadcast you didn't get, you may have been filtered out — usually by division or registration question answer.

## When Will I Get Broadcasts?

Common moments organizers send broadcasts:

| Phase | Typical broadcast |
| --- | --- |
| **Registration open** | Welcome message, what to expect |
| **One week out** | Heat list published, equipment reminders |
| **Day before** | Floor plan, parking, check-in time |
| **Event morning** | Briefings, weather updates, rule reminders |
| **During the event** | Schedule changes, score corrections, finalist announcements |
| **Post-event** | Awards, photos, next-event teaser |

Treat the **Announcements** tab as your primary source of truth — competition information posted in Slack, Instagram, or the host gym's group chat may not match what's official.
