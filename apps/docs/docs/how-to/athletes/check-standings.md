# How to Check Your Division Standings

View your ranking and scores during a competition.

## Steps

1. Open the competition page
2. Click **Leaderboard**
3. Select your division from the dropdown
4. Find your name in the rankings

## Reading the Leaderboard

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Rank** | Your overall position |
| **Athlete** | Competitor name |
| **Points** | Cumulative ranking points |
| **Event Scores** | Individual event results |

## Leaderboard Updates

Scores update in real-time as judges enter results. Refresh the page to see the latest standings.

## Filtering Options

- **Division** - View specific division
- **Event** - See event-by-event results
- **Top 10** - Quick view of leaders
