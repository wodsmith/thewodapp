# How to Update Your Registration

Modify your registration details or division selection after registering.

## Prerequisites

- Active registration for a competition
- Registration changes still allowed (check with organizer)

## Updating Personal Information

1. Navigate to the competition page
2. Click **My Registration**
3. Click **Edit Registration**
4. Update your information
5. Click **Save Changes**

## Changing Your Division

If division changes are permitted:

1. Go to **My Registration**
2. Click **Change Division**
3. Select your new division
4. Confirm the change

:::caution
Division changes may require organizer approval and could affect your entry fee.
:::

## Canceling Registration

To cancel and request a refund:

1. Go to **My Registration**
2. Click **Cancel Registration**
3. Confirm cancellation

Refund policies vary by competition. Check the competition details for the refund policy.
