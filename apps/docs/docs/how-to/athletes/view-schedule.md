# How to View Your Heat Schedule

Find your assigned heat times and lane assignments for an in-person competition.

## Prerequisites

- Registered for an in-person competition (online competitions don't have heats)
- Heat assignments have been published by the organizer

## Steps

1. Sign in and navigate to the competition page.
2. Click the **Schedule** tab.

If you're signed in, your heats are highlighted as **My Heats** at the top of the page so you don't have to scroll to find yourself.

![Athlete schedule with My Heats highlighted](/img/how-to/athletes/athlete-schedule.png)

## Reading the Schedule

Each heat card shows:

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Event** | Workout name (e.g., "Event 01: Fran") |
| **Date and start time** | When to be on the floor |
| **Heat number** | Your group within the event |
| **Lane** | Your assigned lane / station |

Below your personal heats, the full event schedule is grouped by event so you can see what's happening on the floor at any given time.

## Check-In Reminder

Arrive at your lane **at least 10 minutes before your heat start time**. Late arrivals can forfeit the heat — once the clock starts, judges typically don't restart for missing athletes.

## Schedule Changes

Organizers occasionally adjust heats — combining lanes, adding heats for late registrations, shifting times for venue logistics. When changes happen:

- The schedule page updates immediately
- The organizer typically sends a broadcast (see the **Announcements** tab)

Re-check the schedule the morning of the event and again before each event. The clock at the top of the page is the live server time, not your device's clock — use it to gauge how long until your next heat.

## My Heats Section

The **My Heats** section at the top is the most important thing on this page. It only shows heats you're personally assigned to and re-orders them in chronological order, so the next heat is always at the top.

If you're a partner / team registration, both teammates see the same heat assignments.
