# How to View Your Heat Schedule

Find your assigned heat times and workout order for a competition.

## Prerequisites

- Registered for a competition
- Heat assignments have been published by the organizer

## Steps

1. Navigate to the competition page
2. Click **Schedule** or **My Schedule**
3. Locate your name in the heat listings

If you're signed in, your heats are highlighted automatically.

## Understanding the Schedule

Each heat listing shows:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Heat Number** | Your group (e.g., "Heat 3") |
| **Event** | Which workout |
| **Start Time** | When to be on the floor |
| **Lane** | Your assigned lane or station |

## Check-in Reminder

Arrive at your lane **10 minutes** before your heat start time. Late arrivals may forfeit their heat.
