# How to Sign Up as a Volunteer

Apply to volunteer or judge for a WODsmith competition.

## Prerequisites

- A WODsmith account (any user can volunteer; no special role required)
- An invite link from the organizer, **or** a public competition page with volunteer signup enabled

## Direct Invite Flow

If the organizer added you to the **Volunteers → Roster** with your email, you'll get an email titled "You're invited to volunteer at [Competition Name]".

1. Click the link in the email.
2. Sign in if prompted, using the email the organizer invited.
3. Review the prefilled form.

![Volunteer signup form](/img/how-to/judges/judge-volunteer-page.png)

4. Fill in any optional details:
   - **Phone Number** — for day-of coordination
   - **Certifications / Credentials** — CrossFit L1 Judge, L2, EMT, First Aid/CPR, etc.
   - **Custom registration questions** — set by the organizer (T-shirt size, dietary preferences)
5. Click **Submit Application**.

## Public Signup Flow

When the organizer hasn't sent a direct invite, the competition's public page shows a **Sign Up to Volunteer** button on the right rail.

1. Open the competition page (e.g., `/compete/{slug}`).
2. Click **Sign Up to Volunteer**.
3. Sign in or create an account.
4. Complete the same form shown above.

## What Happens Next

Your application enters the organizer's **Volunteers → Roster** with status **Application**. The organizer reviews and approves it, optionally assigning roles like **Judge**, **Scorekeeper**, **Medical**, or **Emcee**.

Once approved:

- You appear on the public roster (if the organizer enabled this)
- You can be assigned to **Shifts** (time-based work blocks) and **Heats** (judge rotations)
- You'll see additional menu items on the competition page based on your role:
  - Judges with score-input access see **Review** (online) or score-entry deep links (in-person)
  - All approved volunteers see their assigned shifts and heat assignments

## Editing Your Application

If you need to update your information after submitting, contact the organizer. Email and name changes that affect billing or identity require the organizer to re-issue the invite.
