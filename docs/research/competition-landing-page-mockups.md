# Competition Landing Page Mockups

**Project:** WodSmith Competition Landing Pages
**Based on:** Competition Corner Analysis
**Date:** November 25, 2025

---

## Design Principles

1. **Information hierarchy** - Most important info visible immediately
2. **Progressive disclosure** - Details expand on demand
3. **Mobile-first** - Responsive from the start
4. **Action-oriented** - Clear CTAs for registration
5. **Real-time** - Live updates for schedule/leaderboard

| Tablet     | 640-1024px | Two columns, condensed sidebar |

## Animation Guidelines

1. **Page transitions:** Fade in (150ms)
2. **Accordion expand:** Slide down (200ms ease-out)
3. **Tab switch:** Fade crossfade (100ms)
4. **Hover states:** 150ms transition
5. **Loading states:** Skeleton pulse animation
6. **Live updates:** Subtle highlight flash on data change
