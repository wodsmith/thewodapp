The check-in kiosk is a public page volunteers run from any tablet or laptop — no organizer account needed.

**Setting up:**

1. Click **Open check-in kiosk** — it opens in a new tab (this dashboard stays put). Hand that device to whoever works the door.
2. Volunteers can also open it themselves: anyone with a volunteer role on this competition gets a Check-In button on the public competition page and their volunteer dashboard.

**How the kiosk works:**

- It's search-first: type any athlete's name or email — for team divisions, *any* teammate's name finds the team.
- One tap on **Check in team** checks in the whole registration. Undo is available on the card.
- Each athlete's row shows their waiver status. If someone hasn't signed, tap **Sign on iPad** and hand them the device — the signature is recorded under their own name.
- The banner tracks checked-in vs total and how many teams still owe waivers.

**Tracking:** a "Checked In" column with timestamps appears on the Athletes page and in its CSV export.

Check-in is for in-person competitions only.
