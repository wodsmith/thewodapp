This preview is your private, complete view of standings. Athletes never see it.

**The preview shows everything:**

- Scores on **draft (unpublished) events**
- **Unpublished division results**
- A column for **every event**, scored or not (unscored cells show "—")

**The public leaderboard** (`/compete/your-slug/leaderboard`) only shows published events and published division results. If the preview looks right but the public board looks empty, you haven't published yet — do that per event + division from the **Results** page.

**For online competitions**, each scored cell carries a review-status badge and links straight to that submission's review page. Team cells show a reviewed count (e.g. "1/2") so you can spot half-reviewed pairs. Use this as your worklist: when every cell shows verified/adjusted and standings look right, publish.

Rankings here use your configured scoring algorithm, so point totals match exactly what the public board will show after publishing — including athletes without a score, who tie one place behind the last scored athlete in their division.
