This page edits one registration end to end. What you can change depends on whether the athlete has claimed their account yet.

**Always editable:**

- **Team name** — edit inline in the registration info card.
- **Division** — Change Division in the header actions.
- **Affiliate** — per member, in each profile card.
- **Registration answers** — per question, per participant, including teammates who haven't accepted their invite yet (their answers pre-fill the sign-up form).

**Name and email** are only editable while the athlete is an unclaimed placeholder (registered manually or via invite, hasn't signed up). Once they claim their account, those fields lock — the athlete owns their profile. You'll see a "Name and email are locked" note.

**Team rosters** (team divisions):

- **Add Teammate** — from the header button or any open-slot card; sends an invite email immediately.
- **Pending invites** — resend or cancel from the dashed invite cards. "Awaiting response" means no reply yet; "Awaiting sign-up" means they submitted the form but haven't created an account.
- **Remove** a teammate from their profile card.

**Waivers are read-only** — signatures are legal records and can't be edited. Pre-signed waivers from pending invitees show a "Pre-signed" badge.

**Scores and videos** for each of the athlete's events are at the bottom. Deleting a score here only affects this registration's division — if the athlete is also registered in another division, that score is untouched.
