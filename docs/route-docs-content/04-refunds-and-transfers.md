All three actions live in the row's actions menu (⋯), or on the athlete's detail page.

**Refund a registration:**

1. Open the actions menu → Refund. Partial refunds are supported.
2. The refund is paid from **your connected Stripe account**.

> **Money note:** the WODsmith platform fee is not returned on refunds — your net revenue decreases by the refunded amount. The Revenue page shows a Refunds line and per-division refund column so totals reconcile.

**Move an athlete to a different division:**

1. Actions menu → Change Division.
2. Pick the new division. Their scores and submissions follow the rules of the new division.

**Transfer a registration to a different person:**

1. Actions menu → Transfer Registration and enter the recipient's email.
2. The recipient gets a link to accept. Until then the row shows a yellow "Transfer Pending" badge — copy the link from there if they lost the email, or cancel the transfer.

Transfers require a paid registration; manually-added (comped) registrations can't be transferred this way — remove the registration and add the new person instead.

**Remove a registration** frees the spot and cleans up the athlete's scores in that division. Use Transfer instead if someone else is taking the spot.
