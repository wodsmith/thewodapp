Paid registration requires a connected Stripe account — athletes pay at registration and the money goes directly to you.

**If you see "Stripe connection required":**

1. Follow the link to your team's payout settings.
2. Complete Stripe's onboarding (business details, bank account). This is Stripe's identity process and can take a few minutes.
3. Return here — the pricing form appears once the account is verified.

**Set your fees:**

1. Set the **default registration fee**. This applies to every division.
2. Override per division where needed (e.g. team divisions priced per team).
3. Check the **fee breakdown preview** — it shows the WODsmith platform fee and Stripe's processing fee, and exactly what you net per registration.

**Running a free competition?** Leave the fee at $0 — no Stripe account needed, and athletes skip checkout entirely.

To offer discounts, create codes on the **Coupons** page; athletes apply them before checkout. For what happens to fees when you refund, see the note on the Athletes page docs.
