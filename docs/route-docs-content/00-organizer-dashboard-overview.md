The sidebar is organized around the life of your competition:

**Competition Setup** — do these roughly in order before opening registration:

1. **Edit** — name, dates, timezone, registration window.
2. **Divisions** — who can register, team sizes, capacity.
3. **Events** — the workouts athletes perform and how they're scored.
4. **Pricing** — connect Stripe and set fees (skip for free competitions).
5. **Waivers** — legal documents athletes must sign at registration.

**Run Competition** — your event-day tools:

- **Athletes** — every registration: transfers, refunds, rosters, answers.
- **Schedule** — heats, lanes, and venues (in-person).
- **Results** — enter scores and control what's published to the public leaderboard.
- **Leaderboard Preview** — see full standings, including unpublished results, before athletes do.
- **Check-in** — day-of kiosk for in-person competitions.
- **Broadcasts** — message your athletes and volunteers.

**Business** — money and partners: Revenue, Coupons, Sponsors, Co-hosts.

The overview page's quick-action cards walk you through the next setup step for events, heats, and results.

First competition? Follow the [step-by-step tutorial](https://docs.wodsmith.com/tutorials/organizers/first-competition) on our docs site.
