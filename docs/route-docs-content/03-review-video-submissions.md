The submissions list shows one row per registration with review progress. Team rows show a video count — an amber badge like `1/2 videos` means a partner video is missing.

**To review a submission:**

1. Open the submission. Watch the video; your notes and no-rep tallies are saved per video and aggregated for teams.
2. Compare the athlete's claimed score (shown with any tiebreak) against what you observed.
3. Choose one action:
   - **Verify** — the claimed score stands.
   - **Adjust Score** — enter the corrected score. Penalty classification is optional (None / Minor / Major); pick None for a plain correction. On multi-round workouts you can apply a percentage penalty to selected rounds only.
   - **Mark Invalid** — removes the score from the public leaderboard entirely.

**Common situations:**

- **Video but no score** — an Enter Score form appears in the sidebar so you can record it yourself.
- **Broken or missing video link** — edit the URL, or fill an empty teammate slot, in the video links editor below the verification controls.
- **Time-cap workouts** — cap status is derived automatically from the time you enter; a reps-at-cap field appears when the cap is hit.

Verified, adjusted, and penalized statuses all appear on your Leaderboard Preview so you can track what's left to review.
